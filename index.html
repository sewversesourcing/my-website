WHO WE ARE

As a visionary sourcing partner, SewVerse Sourcing navigates the complexities of a shifting market with predictive expertise, seamlessly aligning supply with demand to deliver tailored solutions for apparel, home, accessories ,embroideries and raw materials, while streamlining supply chain operations to unlock new opportunities and drive business growth through increased agility ,reduced costs, and enhanced collaboration with suppliers and manufacturers.

WHAT WE DO

Sustainable Sourcing

With our extensive network of suppliers, we are able to satisfy the diverse needs of our customers keeping in mind the brand's positioning and their quality-price ratio. These factories, located close to our offices, are reliable, work in a transparent manner and are regularly visited and audited.

Designing & Development

With the team of designers we create the styles/designs according to the mood boards, inspirations fromcustomers and market intelligence.

Sampling & Risk Analyses

In order to offer the right product for the right client, we focus on first collecting detailed information from the styling department of each brand.

Understanding the customer's needs and expectations is what sets us apart and helps us to be more efficient in ourresearch of the perfect collection samples.

Production

Once the sample has been accepted, the price negotiated and the order sheet received we can start the production. Throughout this phase we submit the materials and accessories, preproduction, production as well as pre shipment garments. In order to provide the real time progress of these key production stages, and thus minimize risks, our collaborators, in direct contact with the factories,provide a detailed weekly report to our customers.

Logistics

Garments are shipped only after checking that they comply with the customer's requirements. In addition to the approval given at each stage of production, we have a team of technicians who carry out random checks on the production before shipment. Once this has been done, we organize shipping of the goods and follow-up of the merchandise, from the factory to the forwarding agent, taking care to provide all documents needed for clearance.
